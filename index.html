Hi, I’m Sofia! I’m originally from Morocco, now residing outside of DC. I graduated with my degrees in Computer Science and Sales. After several years working in sales i realized that programming is my true passion.

I believe that if we worry about failing, or coming up against obstacles along the way, this can deter us from starting. But all of that is imaginary thinking because we only know what will really happen when we start moving towards our goal and deal with these challenges as we come upon them.

I love programming because solving problems and seeing ideas come to life, especially if it improves people's lives in some way, is really satisfying..

In my free time, I also like seeking out great coffee, hiking and painting.
